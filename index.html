About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-focused initiative working to empower students through education, skill development and community engagement.


We believe every student deserves opportunities to learn, grow and build confidence regardless of their background.


Through awareness programs, volunteer activities and learning initiatives, we aim to create positive social impact and inspire future leaders.


Our vision is to support young minds and help create a stronger and more empowered community.
